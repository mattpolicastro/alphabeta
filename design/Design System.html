Principles
All borders 1.5px dashed. No rounded corners. No drop shadows. The only solid borders are on active/selected states and the locked-bet card.
Narrator = dark ink ground. Product UI = paper ground. Never mix. Test: "would the user toggle this?" → paper. "Is this me explaining?" → dark.
Status is read-only. Badges derive from lifecycle position. The journal reads, it never authors.
Discipline prompts are on-canvas. Dashed terracotta, paper register. They're real product affordances (toggleable), not meta-commentary.
Margin notes = Caveat, terracotta, rotated. Handwritten feel. Sparingly — one per panel max.
Blueprint grid always visible. 28px × 28px, 3.5% ink. Reinforces the wireframe/draft register.
