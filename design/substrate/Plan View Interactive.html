▶ what changed from the flat list
Sequences are visually grouped. The terracotta header row + shared background band says "these bets are one chain" without badges or elbow lines. The dependency is the row order within the group — top to bottom = first to last in the chain.
Standalones sit between groups as plain rows. You can instantly see "email sequence, then the plan-picker (standalone), then checkout sequence, then onboarding (standalone)."
Drag semantics are clear: group headers move the whole sequence; standalone rows move themselves. Bars slide horizontally for rescheduling. Won/running bars are fixed — you can't reschedule what's in flight.
