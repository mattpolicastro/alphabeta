The ledger is the substrate's memory

One record, three lenses. Ledger answers "how is each objective being served by evidence?" — it's the join between the strategy layer and the bets. Board answers "what's in flight?" Log answers "what have we decided, and when?" — the journal, literally.

Because status is derived, the ledger can't be gamed by relabeling. A bet is "won" only because its revisit said so against a pre-registered fold-if. Outcome-as-record, not outcome-as-edit.
