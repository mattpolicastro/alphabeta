Five layers, one discipline. Pre-data (layers 1–3): govern, plan, commit before you can see. The experiment lives and dies (layer 4): method-specific monitoring + verdict. Post-experiment (layer 5): method-agnostic learning, looping back to strategy. Existing tools answer "can I run this?" — alphaBeta surfaces "should I?" and holds you to what you committed.

3 Refinement pre-data
The single-bet lifecycle — from a vague idea to a locked, timestamped commitment. Everything here happens before any data exists. That's the whole discipline: commit before you can see.
1 Bet Front DoorDump → reflect → sharpen into the wager. Scans for the falsifier; mechanism separate from confidence.
2 Feasibility & InstrumentFold-if = detection spec. Live fit engine nudges you to the instrument — nudge, not gate.
3 Decision CriteriaPre-register win / inconclusive / loss actions. Evidence bar supplied by the instrument.
4 Commit & LockDraft / lock. Timestamps, freezes, fingerprints. Immutable — edits make a new version.
4 In-flight → Resolution method-specific
The experiment lives and dies. Method-specific monitoring (where applicable) + the verdict. The bucket is computed from the locked fold-if; the pre-registered action is recalled; deviations are logged. Verdicts propagate back to layer 2 (win unlocks the next step; loss prunes the branch).
↻ Revisit
Commitment meets outcome. Bucket computed from fold-if, action recalled, deviation logged beside the timestamp.

 concept sketch
Monitoring surfaces (A/B: SRM + peek discipline · holdback: novelty window · quasi: pre-trend validation · observational: spec-lock) are conceptualized per instrument but not yet built as standalone views. This is the natural home for all method-specific patterns.
5 Knowledge Management post-experiment
Method-agnostic — by the time you're here, the method doesn't matter. Per-bet learning (calibration, "what I'd bet differently") feeds the next bet. Aggregate learning (patterns across many bets over time) feeds the next cycle. The loop from layer 5 back to layer 1 is what makes the platform a learning system rather than bureaucracy.
Per-bet learning is captured at Revisit. Aggregate learning and the 5→1 loop mechanism are unbuilt. Calibration / track-record is a deferred opt-in — win-rate scoreboards create perverse incentives.
↑ loop — aggregate learning feeds the next cycle's strategy
⛓ Integrity / Chain of Custody · cross-cutting
Not a layer — a property of the substrate that instruments layers 2–5. Timestamps (when locked, when judged) · content fingerprint (proving what was committed) · immutability (locked fields can't be edited) · deviation logging (overrides in the user's own words) · derived status (flows from lifecycle, never set manually). The spine of evidentiary integrity.
