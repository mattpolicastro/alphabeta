Insertion moment: method selection

This is arguably the first discipline intervention — and the one existing tools skip entirely. They assume you've already decided to run an experiment. ⍺lphaβeta asks whether you should, and which kind.

For the diagonal user it's load-bearing: they may not know holdback or quasi-experiments exist. The finder surfaces options their stats background wouldn't — without making them author the choice cold.
