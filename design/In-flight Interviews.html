In-flight: sessions underway

The discipline here is recruitment fidelity and guide adherence — not SRM and peek. Completely different failure modes for a completely different method.

Recruitment drift (interviewing the wrong people) is the qualitative equivalent of a broken randomization. Guide drift (asking unplanned questions) is the equivalent of peeking — it inflates the "evidence" by letting you fish for what you want to hear.

The in-product prompt catches the drift and asks you to mark it, not suppress it. Emergent findings are valuable — they just can't count as pre-registered confirmation.
